Wolffia


A graphical user interface to setup, execute, monitor and analyze molecular dynamics simulations.

Credits

Developers

Wolffia is been developed by the Computational Science Group at the University of Puerto Rico at Humacao. The current members of the group are Carlos J. Cortés Martínez, Frances Martínez Miranda, Mirgery Medina Cuadrado, Melissa López Serrano, José O. Sotero Esteva, Radamés Vega Alfaro. The application includes work done by the following previous members of our group: Myrna I. Merced Serrano and Axel Y. Rivera Rodríguez. (on the use of surnames in Puerto Rico)

Collaborators

This project has been significantly influenced by Michael L. Klein (now at Temple), A.T. Charlie Johnson (UPenn), Robert R. Johnson (UPenn) and Preston Moore (USP).
